About us

Riccarton IT Solutions is a small start up company, created in 2011 by a group of students from Heriot-Watt University, Edimburgh. Studying in the Mathematical and Computer Science departement, the students specialised in either Computer Science or Information Systems.
Since the compagny was founded, the group has taken on a small amount of projects that were given to them, and have managed to meet their client's requirements.
