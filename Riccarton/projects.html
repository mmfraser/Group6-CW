  1. Current Projects

    • CD/DVD sales charts

    Hired by QuickSilver Music, the group was asked to create a website application designed to help our client, owner of QuickSilver Music, as well as his store managers to log and track their sales online in a safe environnement.
    As well as save the sales info, the client asked that the website should be able to create chartsand graphs of these sales, and be able to compare the sales in differente categories (genre, store, artist, CD/DVD).
    A top 10 shart was also generated on a weekly bases to inform subscribed custormers about CD/DVDs that were selling well.
